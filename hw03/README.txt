Hw03 Readme:

SetTemp101.sh:

Sets two temperature sensors on i2c address 0x48 and 0x49 to have 12 bit resolution and an alarm 3 degrees Celsius higher than the current temperature.  The alarm resets when the temperature is below 2 degrees above the initial temperature

TmpRead.js:

Prints the current temperature to start.  Prints the temperature on both TMP101 sensors when an alarm is triggered on either sensor. 

Etch-a-Sketch.js

Uses an 8x8 Bi-Color LED matrix along with 5 buttons to play an etch-a-sketch game on the LED matrix.  Functions of buttons are up, down, left, right, and clear from top to bottom.
